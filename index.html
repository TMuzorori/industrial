What we do best

Industrial raw materials

We operate as suppliers, representatives, agents and stockists of a variety of chemical raw materials. All the Company’s trading activity is centred on the supply of industrial raw materials. These are used in a variety of manufacturing processes in Zimbabwe

We offer Credit facility

For certain product lines we receive stock on consignment from various suppliers enabling us to offer credit to all of our credit worthy customers.

Product warehousing

Our products are stored at Bak Storage in Harare and Bulawayo, as well as our own warehouse in Eastlea, leaving our expert team to concentrate on our core business.

Product sourcing

We are privileged to have a vast supply chain that is supported by a network of Principals and suppliers from global destinations such as China, USA, Europe, RSA, West Africa, Mozambique Malaysia, Vietnam, Thailand and many others.
